About Curry Leaves

Curry Leaves brings the authentic taste of India to Poland with four locations across Warszawa, Gdańsk, and Poznań. Our head chef prepares each dish with traditional spices and time-honored recipes — from fiery curries to aromatic biryanis, crispy street food to tandoori specialties. Every bite tells a story of India’s rich culinary heritage.
