Curry Leaves Menu Audit

Source checked: https://curryleaves.pl/menu/

Result:

- The website menu in `index.html` is a curated subset, not the full official Curry Leaves menu.
- Samosa pricing matches the official menu: 18/20 PLN.
- Several official appetizers are missing: Tandoori Grill Paneer, Tandoori Grill Fish, Tandoori Grill Prawns, Fry 65 Gobi, Fry 65 Fish, Fry 65 Prawns.
- Official classic vegetarian items missing from the current site include Assami Crispy Vegetables.
- Official rice items are missing: Plain Basmati Rice, Lemon Basmati Rice, Jeera Basmati Rice, Coconut Basmati Rice, Tomato Basmati Rice.
- Official biryani has variants and prices: Vegetables 39 PLN, Chicken 42 PLN, Mutton 46 PLN, Fish 42 PLN, Prawns 46 PLN. The current site has simplified `Dum Biryani` and `Vegetable Biryani` items.
- Official breads include more naan/roti variants: Plain, Butter, Garlic, Coconut, Kalaunji, Coriander, Methi, Chili.
- Official extras and drinks are not currently listed: Vegetable Raita, Papadam, Lassi, cold drinks, water.
- Official top curries are structured as a choose-protein plus choose-sauce system. The current site lists selected composed dishes like Butter Chicken and Chicken Tikka Masala.

Recommendation:

Use the current site as the premium short ordering menu for now. Before owner handoff, decide whether to keep this curated menu or rebuild the menu section to match the full official choose-protein/choose-sauce structure.
